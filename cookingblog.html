Banana Walnut Bread

Ingedrients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 3 ripe bananas smashed(about 1 cup)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2-1 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
DIRECTIONS:
  1. Whisk sugar and butter, then add vanilla essence and mix well.
  3. Add mashed banana and milk , mix well.
  5. Add flour, salt, baking soda, walnuts Mix well the dough will be rough with the nuts
  6. Grease the loaf pan with butter Preheat oven with 350 F.
  7. Bake the bread for 50 minutes and Insert a toothpick into the loaf, if it comes out clean the bread is ready.
  8. Turn in on the cooling rack let it cool for 1 hour.
  9. Try, eat and comment!!!
